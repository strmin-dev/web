Google Developer Student Clubs - PUP

Google Developer Groups on Campus Polytechnic University of the Philippines, or GDG PUP for brevity, is a student-driven organization supported by Google Developers, focused on creating a collaborative environment for students to develop their technical skills and grow professionally.

Vision

The Google Developer Student Club (GDSC) at the Polytechnic University of the Philippines (PUP) aims to create a space for students to learn, network, and contribute to technology development.

Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and real-world application, fostering a culture of peer-to-peer learning and innovation.
