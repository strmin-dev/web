PUP Wired

WIRED is a PUP-based organization dedicated to serving the electrical engineering community, the university, and the country. The organization comprises students in pursuit of continuous excellence in the field of electrical engineering, acquiring knowledge and developing skills through training supervised by its very own WIRED Team.

Vision

In accordance with the idealism of the college and the department, the organization envisions itself as a community friendly institution that willingly hones the students' theoretical, practical, and moral skills directed towards the field of Electrical Engineering. The members will render their skills and knowledge acquired to serve and maintain the electrical system of our "Sintang Paaralan."

Mission

The Wired Guild acts as a conduit to generate top caliber and competitive students through comprehensive training, held competitions, and national-scale competitions that strengthens, enriches, and partakes in the specialized knowledge in the application of electrical engineering while remaining morally upright in all circumstances.
